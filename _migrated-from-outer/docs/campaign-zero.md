## The Story

Filipino kids in a remote barangay building skateboards from scrap wood — coconut wood decks, recycled bearings, grip tape from roof sealant and sand. The road to Cloud 9 is six kilometres of cracked asphalt. They skate it every afternoon.

The campaign funds proper skateboard setups, safety gear, a mini ramp, and ultimately a community skatepark plan. The documentary crew (Bren + Rick) is embedded with the community, producing footage that becomes both the campaign content and a standalone film.

## Exchange Tiers

### Premium Tier — Handmade Skateboard (Auction)

- **What:** Original handmade skateboard built by the kids from local materials. Signed.
- **Price model:** Auction. Starting bid £100. Reserve price £200 (covers item + PH→UK sea freight + customs).
- **Supply:** 5 boards per campaign.
- **Shipping:** Sea freight Philippines → UK, ~37 working days. Customs: 12% duty + 20% VAT on declared value. Funder informed pre-purchase.
- **Alternative:** UK/event pickup for auction winners (zero shipping cost).
- **Packaging:** Custom packaging sourced locally. Bubble wrap + reinforced cardboard.

### Standard Tier — Darkroom Print

- **What:** Giclée print on Hahnemühle archival paper. A2. Numbered and signed by Bren.
- **Price:** £45 fixed.
- **Supply:** Unlimited (print-on-demand).
- **Fulfillment:** Prodigi or Gelato API. 3-5 working days production, 5-14 days shipping. Ships worldwide.
- **Cost to platform:** ~£8-15 per print (unframed, incl. shipping). Margin covers platform ops.
- **QR code on reverse:** V2 feature. Links back to the campaign story.

### Personal Tier — Thank-You Card

- **What:** Handwritten thank-you card from the kids. Written as part of educational activities (English practice).
- **Price:** £15 fixed.
- **Supply:** Scales with community capacity.
- **Fulfillment:** Coordinated by Bren on the ground. Scanned and emailed as digital backup if physical shipping delayed. Physical cards shipped in batches via international mail.

### Supporter Tier — Name on the Wall

- **What:** Name on the community wall + story updates access. No physical exchange.
- **Price:** Donation (any amount, minimum £5).
- **Supply:** Unlimited.
- **Fulfillment:** Instant. Name appears on community wall after payment confirms.
- **Tax note:** This is a donation, not a commercial sale. Different VAT treatment.

## Risk Mitigations (Campaign-Specific)

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Consent forms not completed before trip | **Launch blocker.** No content goes on platform without signed consent. Trip rescheduled if necessary. |
| Shipping costs for boards exceed 50% of auction value | Reserve price set at £200 minimum. UK/event pickup option offered. |
| Rain delays construction | Buffer built into schedule. Honest updates if delayed — authenticity is a feature. |
| Low conversion from personal network | Track stranger ratio from day 1. If 80%+ are known contacts at day 7, pivot distribution strategy. |
| Documentary footage not ready for content unlocks | Content unlock pieces planned and partially captured before campaign goes live. 25% teaser must be ready at launch. |
